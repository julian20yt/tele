Meet Squirmie!

Who is Squirmie?

Squirmie is a red and yellow slime known for his quirky teaching style at the slime school. He only speaks in Blorps and Glorps, but everyone seems to understand him anyway!

Personality

  • Chaotic but lovable
  • Always up for a prank
  • Teaching... something. It's never clear, but it's always entertaining!

Fun Facts

  • Squirmie's lessons usually end with someone covered in slime.
  • Has an uncanny ability to prank Julian20 when he least expects it.
  • Favorite phrase: "Blorp Glorp Blorp!"
